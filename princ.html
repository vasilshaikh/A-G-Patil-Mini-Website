Patil, with the purpose of encouraging students to be a part of innovative technical field. This institute offers diploma engg. Courses in Mechanical Engineering, Civil Engineering, Electronics & Tele-communication Engineering, Computer Engineering and Information Technology . Progress of the institute and welfare of the students will always be my prime duty.
